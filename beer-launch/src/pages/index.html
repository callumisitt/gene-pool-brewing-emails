D.N.Ale is here! The first ever batch of Gene Pool beer will be available in select pubs and inns in the coming days.

We have spent years crafting the best ales possible on a Homebrew level and are proud to finally be able to share these beers with you.

D.N.Ale is a traditional English ale. With delicious nutty notes, hints of hedgerow fruits and a medium sweet malt backbone, D.N.Ale is sure to suit every generation.

Please let us know exactly what you think when trying this first beer in our genealogy. Hit us up with reviews on Facebook, Twitter and Untappd and take pictures of yourself with a pint for Instagram.

We can't wait for you to try it. We can't wait for you to join the Gene Pool!
